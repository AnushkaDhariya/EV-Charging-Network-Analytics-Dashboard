## 📌 Executive Summary
An end-to-end interactive **Power BI Executive Dashboard** designed to analyze electric vehicle (EV) charging network efficiency, financial metrics, and operational performance. Built with a sleek **Dark Mode UI/UX layout**, this analytics tool delivers fast business insights on session volumes, revenue generation, and station-level utilization.

## 💡 Key Highlights & Insights

* 🎯 **Key Metrics (KPI Cards):** Instant tracking of **Total Energy Consumed (kWh)**, **Total Session Revenue (INR)**, and **Total Session Volume**.
* 🏙️ **City Performance (Top N Filtering):** Breakdown of high-performing locations led by **Gurugram**, **Mumbai**, and **Noida**.
* ⚡ **Infrastructure Breakdown:** Volume distribution across **DC_UltraFast**, **AC_Fast**, and **AC_Standard** chargers.
* ⏱️ **Revenue Window Analysis:** Energy consumption dynamics across **Peak Hours vs. Off-Peak Hours**.
* 🗺️ **Station-Level Treemap:** Visual mapping of top-earning charging stations (e.g., Cyber Hub, BKC Tech Park, Expressway Plaza).
* 📈 **Usage Trends:** Daily energy consumption timeline tracking grid demand fluctuation.
